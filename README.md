## 🌟 The Vision

Traditional AI interview systems are usually limited to a single chatbot with no collaboration, organizational memory, or executive-level reasoning. Real companies operate differently — decisions involve HR, technical leadership, finance, and executive management working together.

**So instead of building another AI assistant, we built an entire AI corporation.**

Synapse Corp AI simulates a real enterprise hiring ecosystem where autonomous AI executives collaboratively evaluate candidates like a real company. It moves beyond traditional single-chatbot systems into a realistic AI-driven corporate ecosystem powered by autonomous agents, organizational memory, multimodal intelligence, and real-time executive decision-making.

---

## ✨ Core Innovations

### 1️⃣ Multi-Agent Executive Board
Our system contains multiple specialized AI executives:
- **Ava (HR):** Evaluates communication skills, behavioral responses, and organizational fit.
- **Leo (Developer):** Assesses coding skills, architecture, and technical problem-solving.
- **Chloe (Marketing):** Evaluates brand understanding and strategic messaging.
- **Miles (Accountant):** Analyzes financial acumen.
- **Orion (CTO):** Evaluates technical leadership and system design.
- **Nova (CFO):** Analyzes budget impact, salary expectations, and financial risk.
- **Atlas (CEO):** Makes the final strategic hiring decision based on collective AI reasoning.

### 2️⃣ Cross-Agent Organizational Memory
One of our key innovations is cross-agent organizational memory. Every AI executive inherits context, interview history, and behavioral insights from previous interview stages, enabling continuous and context-aware enterprise evaluation. *The CTO and CFO don't start from scratch—they reference HR observations and previous answers!*

### 3️⃣ Executive Hierarchy & Synthesis
The architecture is divided into three levels:
1. **Operational Agents:** Perform specialized, domain-specific evaluations.
2. **Executive Synthesis Agents:** Analyze organizational insights, risks, and ROI.
3. **The CEO Agent:** Synthesizes all executive feedback and generates the final hiring recommendation using collaborative multi-agent reasoning.

---

## 🚀 The Interview Flow
1. **Initial Screening:** The interview begins with the HR executive agent, Ava.
2. **Specialized Rounds:** The candidate moves through a sequential executive interview pipeline where every AI agent contributes structured feedback.
3. **Executive Analysis:** Each executive agent generates structured assessments including technical evaluation, communication analysis, strategic thinking, and organizational suitability.
4. **Final Decision:** The CEO agent synthesizes all feedback and generates a final hiring recommendation (e.g., *"Recommended for advanced technical leadership track"*).

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

Synapse Corp AI uses distributed asynchronous workflows and scalable AI orchestration to support real-time enterprise-style interactions.

- **Frontend:** Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Python, FastAPI, WebSockets
- **AI Orchestration:** Google Gemini Pro (Multimodal Reasoning)
- **Voice AI:** Speechmatics (Real-time transcription)
- **Database / Memory:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Deployment & Infrastructure:** Docker, Vultr Cloud Compute, Caddy (Reverse Proxy HTTPS), Vercel
